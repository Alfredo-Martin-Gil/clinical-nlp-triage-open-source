# Sepsis — Triage Rules

IF any sepsis Structured Red Flag present  
THEN high  
RATIONALE: Indicates possible sepsis or shock.

IF no red flags AND ≥1 sepsis Risk Factor present  
THEN intermediate  
RATIONALE: Increased risk of systemic infection.

IF no red flags AND no risk factors  
THEN low  
RATIONALE: Localized or mild infection likely.
