# Abdominal Pain — Triage Rules

IF any abdominal pain Structured Red Flag present  
THEN high  
RATIONALE: Possible surgical or life-threatening abdominal pathology.

IF no red flags AND ≥1 abdominal pain Risk Factor present  
THEN intermediate  
RATIONALE: Increased risk of significant intra-abdominal disease.

IF no red flags AND no risk factors  
THEN low  
RATIONALE: Likely benign or self-limited condition.
