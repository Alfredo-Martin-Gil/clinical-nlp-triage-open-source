# Chest Pain — Triage Rules

IF any chest pain Structured Red Flag present  
THEN high  
RATIONALE: Red flags may indicate ACS, aortic pathology, PE, or shock.

IF no red flags AND ≥2 chest pain Risk Factors present  
THEN intermediate  
RATIONALE: Risk factors increase likelihood of serious cardiac cause.

IF no red flags AND <2 risk factors  
THEN low  
RATIONALE: Lower pre-test risk in the absence of high-risk features.
